2. <span data-ttu-id="b17f9-109">Nav esquerda, escolher **dispositivos** \> **Gerir**.  </span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b17f9-109">On the left nav, choose **Devices**  \> **Manage**.</span></span>
